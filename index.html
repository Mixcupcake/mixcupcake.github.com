    Ordering

    Price

    Cost per cupcake is $2.50 or $26.50 per dozen. Cost for mini cupcakes are $11.00 per dozen. Please call us a (775) 329-1748 for pricing on specialty cupcakes with fondant. Gluten free and Vegan available by special order. Please contact us by phone (775) 329-1748 or e-mail us for special orders. We are happy to do cupcakes for baby showers, birthday parties, office parties/luncheons, holiday parties, dinner parties and weddings. Boxed mini cupcakes in either one dozen or two dozen are also available for special orders, just ask! Delivery is now available to most zip codes in the Reno/Sparks area. Orders must be a dozen or more cupcakes to qualify for delivery (or two dozen minis). Advance notice would be great. A small delivery fee will be added to your order.
